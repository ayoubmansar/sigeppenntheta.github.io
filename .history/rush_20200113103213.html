Become a SigEp

Strive to be well rounded, direct and grow your passion.

At the beginning of each semester, we hold what is called formal rush. This consists of three rush events at our house on campus, followed by three invite only callback events. If you have proven yourself as someone who fits the mold of a SigEp, we will issue you a bid through the university.

$750 Balanced Man Scholarship

For students already exemplifying balance, we offer a scholarship to support them in whatever way possible. No obligations to join SigEp, no strings.
