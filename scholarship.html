Join a Brotherhood for Life

We are a diverse group of students from very different financial backgrounds committed to lowering the barrier of entry to the SigEp experience.

We get it, Greek Life is Expensive

Everyone’s financial situation is different; we’re committed to listening and accommodating those in need— This is one of the many reasons you join the only Greek organization on campus with a scholarship fund dedicated to making our brotherhood more accessible. If you're a current brother, or a prospective brother, reach out to the current Vice President of Finance (VPF) to discuss the possibility of need based scholarship award.

Give Back

The primary ways our financial aid fund gets replenished each year is through the generous contributions of our alumni who are committed to the longevity and accessibility of SigEp Penn Theta. Contributions are mangaged by the Sigma Phi Epsilon Penn Theta Alumni and Volunteer Corporation (AVC) and will exclusivley be used making SigEp more accessible for seeking to better themselves through brotherhood. Give the gift of SigEp to someone new.
