Join a Brotherhood for Life

We are a diverse group of students, from very different financial backgrounds, committed to lowering the barrier of entry.

We get it, Greek Life is Expensive

Everyone’s financial situation is different; we’re committed to listening and accommodating those in need. This is one of the many reasons you join the only Greek organization on campus with a financial aid fund dedicated to making our brotherhood more accessible. If you need a little help in paying, fill out our application and we’d love to discuss the possibility of a financial aid award.

  • Apply Here

Give Back

In addition to fundraising, one of the main ways our financial aid fund gets replenished each semester is through donation. Any amount is greatly appreciated. We make the promise that your money will be used to help out a young man seeking to better himself through brotherhood. Give the gift of SigEp to someone new.
